About DEV.TO

Built by developers, for developers

DEV is a community of software developers getting together to help one another out. The software industry relies on collaboration and networked learning. We provide a place for that to happen.

Our Mission

To provide a space where developers can share their knowledge, build their personal brand, and grow together as a community.

Open Source

Forem is the open-source software that powers this community. You can contribute to our codebase and help shape the future of online communities.

Why join DEV?

  • Share your expertise with a global audience of developers.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies.
  • Connect with like-minded individuals and professional opportunities.
